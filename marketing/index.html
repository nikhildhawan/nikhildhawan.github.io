-- About Me --

I started my computer learning with printing star pyramids using for loops in C++ in school. Soon I found my passion in building small programs using nested if else’s, which built my enough interest to study computer science and engineering. I started my career with India’s biggest Service Company, Tata Consultancy Services. Along with my assigned job I started writing automation scripts, which helped my team to do work much faster.

After working for more than one and a half year, I decided to go for Masters in IT to understand advanced fundamentals of Software Engineering. During my Masters I was able to work on much better academics projects and was able to join an Internship program with Yahoo!

In Yahoo! I worked on a flexible asynchronous request processor framework to enable users to schedule and execute ad hoc scripts in multiple languages. My team was impressed with my work and they offered a full time offer after completion of my Internship, along with ownership of development of a very important module.

In Yahoo I was allowed to put on multiple hats. I learnt multiple languages and tools and worked as Developer, Tester, and few bits of System Admin for the projects assigned. Overall did lots of coding and learning. Currently my project is on Big Data stack processing more than 25GB of data per hour.
